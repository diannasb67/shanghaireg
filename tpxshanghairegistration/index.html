Congratulations on becoming a specially selected winner for this prestigious
2018 President's Club - May 5-9, 2019!!

We value your contribution to TPx Communications success and invite you to celebrate your outstanding achievement by joining us on an amazing and customized deluxe trip, to Shanghai, China.

President's Club winners and their guests will stay at the Four Seasons Shanghai, which is ideally located within the historic downtown district of Puxi where the East meets West, offering a tranquil sanctuary for you to experience this “Pearl of China” known as Shanghai. As a Presidents Club winner, you and your guest will be hosted on an exciting adventure; inclusive of roundtrip coach airfare and private deluxe ground transportation in Shanghai, hand selected guest room accommodations at Four Seasons Shanghai (4 nights); inclusive of all taxes and gratuities. Enjoy a hosted daily breakfast, along with at leisure activities and group dinners along with ample time to explore the city on your own. A hosted, custom designed tour or leisure activity is included to enrich your Asian holiday. You will also have an opportunity to experience a unique group activity, with your colleagues. To begin your adventure, please complete the registration page. Once we receive your registration by email, our travel team will be contacting you with the air travel options available for you and your guest. If you choose to extend your stay past the program dates, mark those date changes on your registration form and we will assist you in creating this amazing escape.

You may contact Dianna Sutton Brown for travel related questions at diannasb@corporateplanners.com, her phone contact: 949-493-2545 Ext. 115

Note: U.S. Departures are on May 4th, 2019.
